## Overview

A complex temporal visualization that overlays public opinion trends with historical political eras (Presidential terms).

## Features

- **Contextual Shading**: Background bands represent different presidential administrations.
- **Grouped Trends**: Supports multiple series (e.g., by Political Party).
- **Statistical Rigor**: Includes confidence intervals for all data points.
