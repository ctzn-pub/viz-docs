## Overview

The `DualAxisChart` is essential for visualizing two different time series that share a temporal dimension but have vastly different scales. 

## Features

- **Independent Y-Axes**: Left and right axes for different units.
- **Synchronized X-Axis**: Ensures temporal alignment.
- **Responsive Legend**: Clearly identifies each series and its corresponding axis.

## When to Use

- Comparing **Price vs. Volume**.
- Visualizing **KPIs with different units** (e.g., Dollars vs. Percentages).
