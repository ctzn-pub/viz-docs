## Overview

A high-level overview of health or economic indicators broken down by demographic categories (Age, Race, Income, etc.).

## Interactive Features

- **Toggle Views**: Users can switch between scatter plots, line charts, and bar charts.
- **Uncertainty Layer**: Displays 95% confidence intervals to ensure statistical rigor.

---

> [!NOTE]
> This component requires a structured payload with pre-calculated confidence intervals.
