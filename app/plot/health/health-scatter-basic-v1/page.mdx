## Overview

A foundational scatter plot for examining the relationship between health metrics, such as Obesity and Diabetes prevalence.

## Visual Grammar

- **X-Axis**: Typically represents the independent indicator.
- **Y-Axis**: Represents the outcome indicator.
- **Color**: Can be used to encode geographic regions or demographic tiers.
